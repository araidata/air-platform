# Bias and Civil-Rights Assessment Support

Phase 6 adds public-sector civil-rights review support without adding automated demographic inference or fairness model training.

## Implemented Support

- Civil-rights assessment templates through assessment profiles.
- English/Spanish language-access scenarios.
- Human appeal-path validation checks.
- Fairness-oriented finding categories through scan types and seeded findings.
- Fairness evidence using the existing evidence table.
- AIRB civil-rights, accessibility, language-access, fairness, human-review, and appeal-path indicators.
- Explainable score impacts through existing Bias/Civil Rights, Explainability, and Governance Evidence scoring.

## Operating Boundary

The platform records review evidence, findings, score impacts, and board readiness. It does not make autonomous legal conclusions, infer protected-class status, or run statistical fairness pipelines.

## Evidence Expectations

- Bilingual response comparisons.
- Translated response evidence.
- Escalation and appeal screenshots or notes.
- Accessibility notice evidence.
- Human review workflow evidence.
- Fairness reviewer notes.
