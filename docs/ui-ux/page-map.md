# Page Map

These are the intended initial application pages.

## 1. Executive Dashboard

Summary of overall risk, approvals, open findings, evidence completeness, and review status.

## 2. AI Inventory

Table of AI systems with department, owner, purpose, risk tier, approval status, assessment status, and open findings.

## 3. Assessment Workspace

Workspace for creating and reviewing assessments, mock scan results, checklist results, notes, and evidence.

## 4. Findings Queue

Operational queue for triage, assignment, remediation, risk acceptance, retesting, and closure.

## 5. System Detail Page

Single-system view showing inventory metadata, risk score, findings, evidence, assessments, approvals, and history.

## 6. Bias & Civil Rights Dashboard

Focused view for rights-impacting systems, language access, appeal paths, protected class concerns, and fairness findings.

## 7. Security Findings Dashboard

Focused view for prompt injection, tool misuse, data leakage, supply chain, agent safety, and scanner findings.

## 8. Evidence & Audit Page

Evidence library with filters by system, assessment, finding, evidence type, and audit packet inclusion.

## 9. AI Review Board Queue

Queue of systems ready for review, blocked systems, approved exceptions, and decision history.

## 10. Reports Page

Operational reports, executive summaries, audit packet generation, and export history.

## 11. Settings / Integrations Page

Scanner configuration, export settings, OneTrust planning fields, framework mappings, and future integration settings.
