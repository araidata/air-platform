# Operational Philosophy

AI Assessment Scanner should be practical for a county team to run, inspect, back up, and hand off.

## Principles

- Assessment-first.
- Testing-first.
- Evidence-first.
- API-first.
- Simple runtime.
- Clear operator workflows.
- No fabricated operational data.

## What The Platform Should Optimize For

- Fast assessment intake.
- Repeatable automated tests.
- Preserved raw evidence.
- Clear findings and remediation state.
- Human review decisions with traceable rationale.
- Executive reports based on real records.

## What The Platform Should Avoid

- Infrastructure complexity before operational need.
- Broad scanner breadth before adapter quality.
- Marketing-style UI.
- Chatbot-first workflows.
- Reports that cannot be traced back to evidence.
